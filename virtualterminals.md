I booted my Linux Computer and left my desk for a while: without logging in. Coming back after a while, I noticed that except for the mouse pointer, I could not notice anything on the screen. </br>  
Initially I thought that the only way to come out of this situation was to press and hold the power button and force shutdown my computer. </br>  
Only a minute later I realised that I can login to another **Virtual Terminal**. So I pressed **CTRL+ALT+F3**, logged in, and executed **systemctl reboot**
